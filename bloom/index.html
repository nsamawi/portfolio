Illustration Drawing Painting About

Bloom

Inspired by plants I passed by on daily walks around the D.C. area, these illustrations celebrate splashes of color and delight in the midst of an otherwise grim year. What initially seemed like a constraining period of time turned into the much-needed space to create again. Sometimes it's only within certain limitations that we find opportunities to grow, to develop previously neglected parts of ourselves, to gain a renewed appreciation for the little things in life.
