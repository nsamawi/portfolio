College Posters

As a Resident Advisor in college, I both helped plan academic programs and designed posters to advertise them. Every Residential Hall belongs to a community called a ResCollege, each with its own colors, symbols, and traditions. Brookings, the ResCollege where I lived and worked, has traditions like Breakfast for Dinner and "Brookies" (brownie cookies), Kings as our mascot, and Royal Blue and Yellow as our colors. These posters combined playful imagery and the bright ResCollege colors to entice first-year students to attend.
