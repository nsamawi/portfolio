Loss

This series emerged out of the devastating end of a long and close relationship, each image depicting a different aspect of how losing someone important to you can disrupt so much of what you once thought, believed, and held to be true. Both confrontational and vulnerable, in these pieces I directly address the other person for the pain they caused while openly expressing how much they meant to me.
