Wine

© Copyright 2021 – Designed and Developed by Neema Samawi

Created for Lynfred Winery, a local winery in the Chicago area, these paintings celebrate the beauty of their wines through rich colors, sparkling glasses, and intricate details. In addition to making the paintings themselves, I composed and shot all of the reference photographs, featuring one red and one fruit wine in the first image, a row of dessert wines in the second, and a glass of white, rosé, and red in the third. (If you happen to be in the area, by the way, their wines are excellent!)
