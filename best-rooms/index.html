Best Rooms Poster

As one of the top-ranked universities for student housing, Washington University in St. Louis offers very pleasent living spaces that residents can easily make their own. This poster was for WashU's first ever Best Rooms Contest, where we invited students to share their decked-out rooms for the chance to win prizes. This simultaneously boosted ResLife's social media presence on campus while showcasing the quality of our spaces to prospective students.

In this illustration, I combined our complementary school colors in a way that contrasted without clashing, and I filled the composition with the very dorm furniture that populated our residential halls. Though I added a few decorative details, I deliberately generalized the space and the characters to allow room for imagination and possibility.
