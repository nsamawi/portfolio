Vote Poster

One of the assignments in a Digital Design class I took was to create a nonpartisan Vote poster, right around the time of the 2016 U.S. Presidential Election. It took me a few iterations on different ideas before I stumbled upon one that really struck me. Though the issue isn't exactly neutral across all political views, I included both parties in the picture, communicating the urgency of the issue while easing some of the tension through the illustration's subtle absurdity.
