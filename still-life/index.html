Still Life

This still life was made for the final assignment in a Foundational Drawing course, where the prompt was literally "whatever you want it to be." So I decided to gather as many shiny objects as I could find and shove them all in one drawing. (And if you're curious, ask me about the story of how I pulled three all-nighters in a row to finish this in time for the final review.)
