Finding Neema

Create an autobiographical comic of your most embarrassing moment: this was the prompt for a final assignment in one of my Design classes. While I've had my fair share of embarrassing moments, and while this was probably not the worst one, I instantly thought back to a field trip for an art class I took senior year of high school, when we visited the nearest state park during the peak of autumn to spend the day painting en plein air.

Though we were specifically instructed NOT to go off on our own, and though I have a terrible sense of direction and, at that time, a potato for a phone and absolutely no cell service in the park, I was so captivated by this gentle waterfall running underneath a bridge that I insisted on sitting there and painting all by myself. Spoiler alert: every single person on that field trip waited on the bus a little too long and got home a little too late, all thanks to me.

I even recreated the scene I painted (see below) in several of the frames. Honestly, getting lost was kind of worth it.
