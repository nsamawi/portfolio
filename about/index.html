About Me

Hi! I'm Neema. I am an illustrator, artist, designer, writer, developer, and overall creative person currently based in Washington, D.C. I completed my undergrad at Washington University in St. Louis – originally with the intention of majoring in Design with a second major in Mathematics. The double-major thing didn't quite work out, so I ended up with a Design minor instead.

I create to capture ideas through imagery, visual metaphors, and artistic techniques, with the hope of sharing these ideas and communicating them in a way that resonates with others. When I'm not drawing, designing, writing, or coding, I'm probably running around the National Mall, making endless Spotify playlists, or photographing salads. You can visit my LinkedIn for a peek into my professional life, and you can reach me at design@neemasamawi.com.
